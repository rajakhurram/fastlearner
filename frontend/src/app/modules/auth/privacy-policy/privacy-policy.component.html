Use of Cookies

FastLearner values your online experience and uses "cookies" to personalize it. A cookie is a text file stored on your hard disk by a web page server. It cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. It is uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie. Cookies save you time by telling the web server that you have returned to a specific page. For instance, when registering with or personalizing the FastLearner site or services, a cookie helps FastLearner recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and more. This eases the use and access of your customized features. Note that FastLearner only accepts cookies. By choosing to use our site, you consent to using cookies.
